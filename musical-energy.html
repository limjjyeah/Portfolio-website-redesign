Musical Energy — sound made visible

music has always felt like the most direct language — it bypasses interpretation and arrives somewhere in the body before the mind can get to it. these works are attempts to translate that arrival into something visual.

not illustrations of songs, not data. more like: what does it feel like to really hear something? what is the shape of a particular kind of listening? the results are abstract because the experience is abstract — colour, pressure, movement, release.
