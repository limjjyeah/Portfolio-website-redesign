hi, i'm jennifer.

i've been designing for 10+ years, working with startups, studios, and companies like fisker, indiegogo, levi's, lego, bcg, and invoy. i've done a bit of everything: brand identities, print things, websites, apps, and decks (too many decks).

i like helping people express their ideas in ways that feel clear, natural, and visually simple.

outside of work i love being a homebody with my family and scrolling through photos of my two sons when they're not around.
