Doing Non-Doing — wu wei

wu wei — acting without forcing. the taoist principle of effortless action, of moving with rather than against. not passivity, but a kind of receptive alertness. not nothing, but not clutching either.

these works were made in that spirit. sitting with materials without agenda. letting the image arrive rather than constructing it. the process reveals what it does — sometimes coherent, sometimes scattered, always more honest than anything planned.
