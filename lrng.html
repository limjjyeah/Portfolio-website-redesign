LRNG is a social enterprise that aims to transform how young people access and experience learning, especially those from underserved communities. Our aim was to develop and design a connected and scaleable technical infrastructure that would empower cities to easily curate, connect, and monitor an ecosystem of "experiences where learning happens" within their communities.

With a team of designers and developers, I helped create LRNG's website along with a set of high level features on the internal side of the platform, making it easier for educators and organizations to create, edit, and approve experiences for youth.
