Surf & Skate — the body in motion

surfing and skating are two of the few things that demand complete presence. the wave doesn't wait. the board doesn't negotiate. there's a particular quality of attention that gets activated when the stakes are physical — a quieting of internal noise that i can't produce any other way.

these images are from that world. water, concrete, gravity. moments where thought stops and movement takes over.
