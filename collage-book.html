Collage Book — found material, assembled

pages made over time from whatever was at hand — cut images, torn paper, paint, tape, text fragments. each spread is a kind of accumulation. not planned but gathered. the book holds memory the way a drawer holds things you can't quite throw away.

collaging for me is a way of thinking without language. moving pieces around until something feels true. there's a particular satisfaction in the way unrelated things start to speak to each other when placed close enough together.

ongoing, 2019–present
